The 45th Legacy

We walked the red paths of Jahangirnagar when the winter mist blurred the lines between reality and dreams. We are the 45th batch—a collection of souls bound not just by an academic calendar, but by the shared rhythm of life at the "Capital of Culture."

"আমাদের এই বন্ধন কেবল স্মৃতির জাবর কাটা নয়, বরং একে অপরের পাশে দাঁড়াবার এক অটুট প্রতিশ্রুতি।"

From the chaotic debates at Bot-tola to the serene silence of the lakes, our journey was painted in the hues of brick-red and leaf-green. We learned that brotherhood isn't about agreeing on everything; it's about standing together when it counts.

Today, as we navigate the complexities of professional life across the globe, the need for a unified platform has never been stronger. This portal is our digital home—a place to archive our history, fuel our career growth, and extend a hand of welfare to those among us in need. Established in 2016, we remain, forever 45.
